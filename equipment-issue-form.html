C — Policy Acknowledgement
By signing this form, I confirm that I have read and understood the IT Equipment & Acceptable Use Policy (Policy 36) and I agree to the following:
  • I will use this equipment only for church-related purposes, as set out in the policy.
  • I will not store personal data outside of church-approved systems (ChurchSuite, Microsoft 365 / Google Workspace).
  • I will keep the device secured with a strong password or PIN and enable automatic screen lock.
  • I will install operating system and security updates promptly and will not install unapproved software.
  • I will not allow family members or third parties regular use of the device.
  • I will report any loss, theft, or suspected data breach to the Operations Manager immediately — not the next working day.
  • I will return the equipment within 7 days of leaving my role, in the same condition as issued (fair wear and tear excepted), and will not wipe or reset the device myself.
  • I understand that the church retains ownership of this equipment at all times.
